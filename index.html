Services

Work With Me

I provide a free 10 minute consultation call for prospective clients. For information on rates and scheduling, please call, text, email, or use the contact form.

Sessions

I provide telehealth services through a secure, HIPAA compliant platform. Standard sessions are 50 minutes in length. I can provide shorter or longer sessions per client request at the time of scheduling. These session rates will be prorated accordingly.

Insurance

I am not accepting insurance at this time. If you wish to file for Out of Network coverage with your insurance provider, I can assist by providing theappropriate documentation.

Payment

I accept credit and debit card payments. All payments are due at the time of the appointment.
